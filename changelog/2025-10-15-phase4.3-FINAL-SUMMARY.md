## What Was Accomplished

### 1. Root Cause Investigation ✅

**Problem Identified**:
- Sequential for-loop in `QwenPipeline::prefillBatch` (lines 2090-2142)
- Processes sequences one-by-one instead of in parallel
- Results in 0.97× speedup instead of target 22× speedup

**Evidence Gathered**:
- Instrumentation showed 99.98% of time is sequential iteration
- Only 6.5ms overhead (negligible)
- Time scales perfectly linearly with batch size
- Mathematical proof: total_time = sum(sequence_times)
